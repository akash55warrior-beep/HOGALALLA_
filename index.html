Vision

Gear up
beyond limits

HOGALALLA started as sketches in an exercise book that didn't look right on anyone else's blanks — so the designs got cut into their own line instead.

No stock rooms, no leftover fabric. Every order is made the day it's placed, printed once, and shipped straight to you from West Bengal. One person, one drop at a time.

FAQ

The stuff people usually DM about first.

How long does delivery take?+

Since every piece is printed to order, expect 5–9 days from confirmation to delivery depending on your pincode.

How do I actually pay?+

Orders are confirmed over WhatsApp right now — payment details get shared directly in the chat once size and address are confirmed.

Can I exchange a size?+

Yes — message on WhatsApp within 3 days of delivery and we'll sort a size exchange before the next print run.

Do you ship outside West Bengal?+

Yes, pan-India shipping is available — just send your pincode when you order to confirm timelines.
